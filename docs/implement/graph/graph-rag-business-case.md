# Graph-Enhanced RAG: Multi-Tenant Relationship Intelligence Platform

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the strategic imperative and technical approach for integrating Apache AGE graph database capabilities with our existing AI-powered MSP platform. The solution transforms traditional document retrieval into relationship intelligence, enabling both platform operators and tenants to discover insights hidden in business relationship networks.
