# Billing & Subscription Management - User Guide

## Getting Started with Your Billing Account

Welcome to the MCP platform billing system! This guide will help you understand and use all the billing features available to manage your subscriptions, payments, and financial information with ease and confidence.

## Account Setup and Configuration

### Setting Up Your Billing Profile

1. **Access Billing Settings**: Log in to your account and navigate to Settings → Billing
2. **Complete Your Profile**: Add your billing address, contact information, and tax details
3. **Choose Your Currency**: Select your preferred currency for all transactions
4. **Set Tax Preferences**: Configure tax settings based on your business location and requirements

### Adding Payment Methods

1. **Go to Payment Methods**: Navigate to Billing → Payment Methods
2. **Add Credit/Debit Card**:
   - Click "Add Payment Method"
   - Enter card details (all data is encrypted and secure)
   - Set as default payment method if desired
3. **Add Bank Account** (for ACH transfers):
   - Select "Bank Account" option
   - Enter routing and account numbers
   - Verify micro-deposits (2 small test deposits)

### Managing Multiple Payment Methods

- **Default Payment Method**: Choose which method to use for automatic payments
- **Backup Methods**: Add alternative payment methods for backup
- **Expiration Reminders**: Receive notifications before payment methods expire
- **Automatic Updates**: Enable automatic card updates when cards expire

## Subscription Management

### Finding Available Plans

1. **Browse Plans**: Go to Billing → Plans to see all available subscription options
2. **Compare Features**: Use the comparison table to evaluate plan benefits
3. **Check Pricing**: View pricing in your preferred currency with applicable taxes
4. **Trial Periods**: Look for plans offering free trials

### Starting a New Subscription

1. **Select Plan**: Choose the plan that best fits your needs
2. **Configure Subscription**:
   - Select billing frequency (monthly, annual)
   - Choose billing date preference
   - Add optional add-ons or features
3. **Review and Confirm**: Check subscription details, pricing, and billing schedule
4. **Activate Subscription**: Confirm payment method and activate your subscription

### Managing Existing Subscriptions

**Viewing Subscription Details**:
- Go to Billing → Subscriptions
- Click on any subscription to see detailed information
- View current billing period, next charge date, and usage statistics

**Upgrading Your Plan**:
1. Select "Change Plan" on your subscription
2. Choose the new plan tier
3. Review prorated charges and effective date
4. Confirm the upgrade

**Downgrading Your Plan**:
1. Select "Change Plan" on your subscription
2. Choose the lower tier plan
3. Note: Changes take effect at the next billing cycle
4. Confirm the downgrade

**Adding Add-ons**:
1. Go to subscription details
2. Click "Manage Add-ons"
3. Select desired add-ons
4. Review additional charges and confirm

**Pausing or Canceling**:
1. Access subscription details
2. Select "Manage Subscription"
3. Choose "Pause" (temporary suspension) or "Cancel" (permanent)
4. Confirm your choice and review any applicable terms

## Invoice and Payment Management

### Understanding Your Invoices

**Invoice Structure**:
- **Header**: Invoice number, date, due date, and total amount
- **Line Items**: Detailed breakdown of charges, quantities, and rates
- **Taxes**: Applicable tax charges with breakdown
- **Payments**: Payment history and remaining balance
- **Notes**: Additional information or terms

**Invoice Statuses**:
- **Draft**: Invoice being prepared, not yet finalized
- **Open**: Invoice finalized and awaiting payment
- **Paid**: Invoice fully paid
- **Overdue**: Payment is past due
- **Void**: Invoice canceled

### Paying Your Invoices

**Automatic Payments**:
- Enabled by default for most subscriptions
- Processed 3 days before due date
- Email confirmation sent when payment succeeds

**Manual Payment**:
1. Go to Billing → Invoices
2. Select "Pay Now" on any open invoice
3. Choose payment method
4. Confirm payment details
5. Complete payment process

**Payment Methods Supported**:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Bank Transfers (ACH/Wire)
- Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Alternative Methods (PayPal, Venmo)

### Managing Failed Payments

**Automatic Retry Logic**:
- Payment automatically retried on days 1, 3, and 7 after failure
- Multiple payment methods attempted if available
- Email notifications sent for each attempt

**Manual Retry**:
1. Go to Billing → Invoices
2. Click "Retry Payment" on failed invoice
3. Update payment method if needed
4. Confirm retry

**Payment Failure Prevention**:
- Keep payment methods updated and current
- Ensure sufficient funds for automatic payments
- Add backup payment methods
- Monitor email notifications for payment issues

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Payment Problems

**Payment Declined**:
- Check card details for accuracy
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Verify card expiration date
- Try alternative payment method
- Contact your bank if issues persist

**International Payment Issues**:
- Verify international transaction permissions
- Check currency conversion settings
- Ensure payment method supports international transactions
- Consider alternative payment methods

### Subscription Issues

**Subscription Not Active**:
- Verify payment method is valid
- Check for failed payments
- Confirm subscription activation
- Contact support if issues persist

**Plan Change Problems**:
- Ensure sufficient permissions for changes
- Check for outstanding balances
- Verify plan eligibility requirements
- Review change restrictions or limitations

### Invoice Discrepancies

**Incorrect Charges**:
- Review invoice details carefully
- Check usage logs for verification
- Compare with plan pricing structure
- Contact billing support for disputes

**Missing Credits or Discounts**:
- Verify discount eligibility
- Check promotional code terms
- Review account credits status
- Confirm automatic discount application
